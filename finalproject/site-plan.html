TradeUp Journey Site Plan

A Personal Trading Challenge

WDD 231

Alejandro

Overview

Purpose

To document and showcase a personal trading challenge where I start with a simple item (a red paperclip) and trade up to a valuable item (a motor). The site will track progress, display the current item, and invite others to participate.

Scenarios

  • User wants to see what item I currently have.
  • User wants to see the history of previous trades.
  • User wants to contact me to offer a trade.

Branding

Website Logo

A simple text-based logo "TradeUp Journey" with a stylized paperclip icon.

Color Palette

Palette URL: https://coolors.co/008080-ff7f50-2f4f4f-f5f5f5

Primary Secondary Accent Background
#008080 #FF7F50 #2F4F4F #F5F5F5
  • Primary (#008080 - Teal): Used for headers, navigation backgrounds, and primary buttons.
  • Secondary (#FF7F50 - Coral): Used for call-to-action buttons (hover states) and highlights to draw attention.
  • Accent (#2F4F4F - Dark Slate): Used for footer backgrounds and text headings.
  • Background (#F5F5F5 - Off-White): Used for the main body background to keep it clean and readable.

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat

Paragraph Font: Roboto

I chose Montserrat for headings because it is modern and bold, reflecting the adventurous nature of the challenge. Roboto is used for body text due to its high readability on screens.
