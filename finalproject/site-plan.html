Alejandro

WDD 231

Site Name

TradeUp Journey

This name clearly communicates the core activity (trading) and the progressive nature of the challenge (journey/up). It implies growth and a story.

Domain: tradeup-journey.com

Site Purpose

The site documents a personal "paperclip challenge" style trading project. It serves as a portfolio of the trades made, showcasing the progression from a low-value starting item to a high-value goal. It also provides a way for interested visitors to propose trades.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A visitor hears about the challenge and wants to know, "What item do you currently have, and what are you looking for next?"
